MAXIMO vs. TRIRIGA focus areas

Many organizations use MAXIMO and TRIRIGA together or transition between them, so understanding their strengths helps you design the right architecture and integration strategy.

Area IBM MAXIMO Application Suite IBM TRIRIGA / MAXIMO Real Estate & Facilities
Primary focus Enterprise asset management, maintenance, and reliability. Real estate, workplace and facilities lifecycle management.
Key strengths Work management, asset registry, inventory, IoT and condition monitoring. Lease accounting, space planning, capital projects, energy and sustainability.
Typical users Maintenance, reliability, operations and asset management teams. Corporate real estate, facilities, finance and workplace experience teams.
Common use with both Unified asset and facility data model, cross‑system workflows and integrated reporting across real estate, maintenance and operations.
